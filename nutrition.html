Why Nutrition Matters?

Proper nutrition fuels your workouts, boosts energy, and helps in muscle recovery. A balanced diet is the key to achieving your fitness goals.

High-Protein Diet Details

This diet plan is designed for muscle growth and recovery, featuring:

  • Daily protein intake: 1.6-2.2g per kg of body weight
  • Lean meats (chicken, turkey, beef)
  • Fish (salmon, tuna)
  • Eggs and Greek yogurt
  • Protein supplements (whey protein)
  • Sample meal: Grilled chicken breast (200g), brown rice (100g), steamed broccoli

Perfect for athletes and bodybuilders looking to maximize muscle gains.

×

Weight Loss Plan Details

This plan focuses on healthy weight loss through calorie control, featuring:

  • Daily calorie deficit: 500-700 calories below maintenance
  • High-fiber vegetables (spinach, kale, broccoli)
  • Lean proteins (chicken breast, fish)
  • Whole grains (quinoa, oats)
  • Healthy fats (avocado, nuts)
  • Sample meal: Grilled salmon (150g), quinoa (100g), mixed greens

Ideal for sustainable fat loss while maintaining energy levels.

×

Balanced Diet Details

This plan promotes overall wellness with a mix of nutrients, featuring:

  • Balanced macros: 40% carbs, 30% protein, 30% fats
  • Whole grains (brown rice, whole wheat)
  • Lean proteins (chicken, tofu, eggs)
  • Healthy fats (olive oil, nuts, seeds)
  • Fruits and vegetables (berries, leafy greens)
  • Sample meal: Baked chicken (150g), sweet potato (100g), sautéed spinach

Suitable for maintaining health and supporting an active lifestyle.
